## Hallucination in Vision-Language Models

My research investigates hallucination problems in Vision-Language Models (VLMs) and develops methods to mitigate them.

### Key Works

**On the Perception Bottleneck of VLMs for Chart Understanding** (2025)

We investigate the perception bottleneck of Vision-Language Models for chart understanding tasks, revealing fundamental limitations in how VLMs process visual information.

**Authors**: **Junteng Liu**, Weihao Zeng, Xiwen Zhang, Yijun Wang, Zifei Shan, Junxian He

[GitHub: Vision4Chart](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Vision4Chart)

---

**In-Context Sharpness as Alerts: An Inner Representation Perspective for Hallucination Mitigation** (ICML 2024)

We propose using in-context sharpness as alerts for hallucination mitigation from an inner representation perspective.

**Authors**: Shiqi Chen, Miao Xiong, **Junteng Liu**, Zhengxuan Wu, Teng Xiao, Siyang Gao, Junxian He
