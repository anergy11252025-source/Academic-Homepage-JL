## LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning

My research in this area focuses on improving the reasoning capabilities of large language models through reinforcement learning and synthetic data generation.

### Key Work

**SynLogic: Synthesizing Verifiable Reasoning Data at Scale for Learning Logical Reasoning and Beyond** (2025)

We propose SynLogic, a method for synthesizing verifiable reasoning data at scale. This work addresses the challenge of obtaining high-quality reasoning data for training LLMs, enabling them to perform better logical reasoning.

**Authors**: **Junteng Liu**, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He
