## LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability

My research explores the internal mechanisms of truthfulness in large language models and develops methods to improve their interpretability.

### Key Work

**On the Universal Truthfulness Hyperplane Inside LLMs** (EMNLP 2024)

We discover a universal truthfulness hyperplane inside large language models that can distinguish truthful from untruthful statements. This finding provides new insights into how LLMs represent and process truthful information internally.

**Authors**: **Junteng Liu**, Shiqi Chen, Yu Cheng, Junxian He

[GitHub: Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane)
