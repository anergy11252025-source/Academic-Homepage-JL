Research Interests
======
* **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Developing methods to enhance logical reasoning capabilities of large language models through reinforcement learning and synthetic data generation.
* **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLMs)**: Investigating perception bottlenecks and hallucination patterns in vision-language models, particularly for chart understanding tasks.
* **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Analyzing internal representations of LLMs to understand and improve their truthfulness, including discovering universal truthfulness hyperplanes.
